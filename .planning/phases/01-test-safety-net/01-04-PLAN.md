## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Committed goldens → CI replay | Frozen JSONL traces are the source of truth a later phase's change is checked against |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-T | Tampering | a future change silently bricks behavior but the trace looks "self-consistent" | mitigate | Standing per-frame invariants (score range, wall-clip, soft-lock) run on EVERY replay (D-11), catching a bricked-but-self-consistent trace the diff alone would miss |
| T-04-T2 | Tampering | randomness/wall-clock sneaks into game/ghost/player and breaks determinism | mitigate | Determinism guard hard-fails CI on forbidden tokens (D-12) |
| T-04-R | Repudiation | a re-bless quietly changes goldens outside the intended region | mitigate | --bless prints a human-readable per-frame/field diff for review before commit (D-07); this is the mechanism that proves Phase 3's "isolated to box edges" |

No new runtime attack surface ships in the .exe — golden traces and tests are dev/test-only artifacts.
</threat_model>
